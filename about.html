About us

The name Nongor came from the Bengali word, নোঙর. Which in English means, the anchor of a ship. At Nongor, we believe travel should be easy, connected, and personal. We started this journey in New Zealand to help people explore the coldest mountains and fabulous film sets without the stress of planning a tour for months, it takes less than 5 minutes too book and hear back from us. We focus on three things, use of smart technology, local secret maps, and flexibility of the tourists. By using Starlink Wi-Fi, we manage to keep you socially active in the wild and at the peak of the mountains. By finding out quiet, less crowded days, we give you the chance to have a peaceful and happy journey. Whether you're a student on a budget, a group of friends, or a solo hiker, we are your anchor for a perfect Kiwi tour.

Team Focus and Mission

our target is to give our customers a stress free and convenient travel experience by bringing people close to nature. We want to make New Zealand's most attractive tourist sites available for all kind of travelers, from a solo travelers to a large groups at a friendly budget. our target is to stay eco-friendly and paperless for easier access to the internet with the help of Starlink. We work hard to find the convenient paths which are less known so that your journey feels easy, hassle free, and smooth.

Why us

We know that planning a trip can be stressful, so we've made everything as simple as possible. Whether you are traveling with a group of 2-4 friends or traveling alone to find some peace. We have your back. our tours are planned to avoid the biggest crowds, and we'll show you unknown, hidden paths and places that most tourists never see. For those who need to stay productive and would like to spend on social media, our buses and mountain hubs have Starlink Wi-Fi and USB-C chargers and other power hubs. You don't even need to buy heavy gears, as we provide our own merchandised jackets and boots in all packages. From easy click away bookings to private group pickups from hotels, we take care of the logistics so you can just enjoy the view and spend time with the other tourists and your friends.
