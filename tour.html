Hobbiton

Single: $3000 Group: $2500 Each

On this guided tour through the famous 12-acre movie set, you finally can enter a real Hobbit Hole. You’ll see the iconic Bag End and the Brand-new decorated interiors on Bagshot Row. We plan our tours in a way so that we can avoid big crowds, so it’s nice and quiet for photo sessions. If you’re coming with a group of 2-4 friends, we make booking easy so you can all sit together for a cold drink at the Green Dragon Inn and enjoy every moments with your friends, we have personal guest gatherings for Classic music and Barbecue. Plus, our bus and the destination have fast Starlink Wi-Fi and USB-C chargers, so you can stay powered up the whole way. There is a supermarket just 5minutes away from the spot, where you can anything you would like to find but cheaper.
